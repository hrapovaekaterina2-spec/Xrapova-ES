🐱 Story: Five Senses

Hello! I’m Marie.
I have five friends — my five senses!

👅 This is my mouth. I can taste ice cream. Mmm! It’s sweet and cold.

👀 These are my eyes. I can see a rainbow. It’s beautiful!

👂 These are my ears. I can hear a bird. It’s singing! Tweet, tweet!

👃 This is my nose. I can smell an onion. Oh no! It’s bad!

✋ These are my hands. I can touch my cat. It’s soft and nice.

I can taste, see, hear, smell, and touch.
Five senses — every day!
